Workshops

3rd - 5th Grade

Rubik's Cube In this Rubik’s Cube workshop, participants will learn the fundamentals of solving the cube, from basic notation to step-by-step solving strategies. We’ll practice hands-on, explore fun cube challenges, and finish with a friendly speed-solving competition. Whether you’re a complete beginner or looking to sharpen your skills, this session is all about learning, playing, and having fun.
Estimathon In this Estimathon workshop, participants will tackle a series of quirky, challenging estimation questions that blend math, logic, and teamwork. Working in groups, they’ll strategize to make the most accurate guesses possible while racing against the clock. It’s a lively mix of problem-solving, collaboration, and friendly competition.
