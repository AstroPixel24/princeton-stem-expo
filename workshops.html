Workshops

3rd - 5th Grade

Estimathon and Games In this math games session, participants will first play Estimathon, where they'll make clever guesses to wacky real-world questions using teamwork and logic. Then they'll jump into a 24 Tournament, racing to combine numbers with addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division to make 24. It's a fast-paced mix of math, strategy, and fun challenges!
Chemistry Lab In this exciting chemistry show, students will watch jaw-dropping live demonstrations, from color-changing reactions to bubbling potions and more. Each experiment highlights the "wow factor" of science, sparking curiosity and wonder while showing how chemistry is all around us.
Marshmallow Engineering In this creative engineering challenge, participants will use marshmallows and spaghetti to build the tallest, strongest tower possible. They'll learn about balance, stability, and teamwork while testing their designs in a fun, hands-on way.
Patterns and Sequences In this math activity, kids will explore cool number patterns and sequences, discovering how numbers connect in surprising ways. Through puzzles, games, and group challenges, they'll practice spotting patterns and predicting what comes next.
Hands-On Biology In this hands-on biology experiment, students will mash strawberries, mix simple household ingredients, and actually pull out strands of real DNA they can see. The process is simplified for younger learners, making molecular biology both fun and memorable.

6th - 8th Grade

Estimathon and Games In this math games session, participants will first play Estimathon, where they'll make clever guesses to wacky real-world questions using teamwork and logic. Then they'll jump into a 24 Tournament, racing to combine numbers with addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division to make 24. It's a fast-paced mix of math, strategy, and fun challenges!
Chemistry Experiments In this hands-on chemistry lab, students will carry out simple but exciting experiments, like mixing baking soda and vinegar or testing chemical reactions. With more responsibility and direct participation, they'll explore how everyday ingredients can reveal the power of chemistry.
Rubiks Cubing In this Rubik's Cube workshop, participants will learn the fundamentals of solving the cube, from basic notation to step-by-step solving strategies. We'll practice hands-on, explore fun cube challenges, and finish with a friendly speed-solving competition. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to sharpen your skills, this session is all about learning, playing, and having fun.

More Hands-On Biology In this biology workshop, students will carry out a real lab experiment: extracting DNA from strawberries using household materials. The activity takes about 40 minutes, giving students time to learn the science behind DNA while seeing it firsthand.
