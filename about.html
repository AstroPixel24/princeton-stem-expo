Our Mission

The Princeton STEM Expo (PSE) is launching as an inaugural STEM Exploration Event dedicated to inspiring and empowering young problem-solvers. Taking place on Saturday, September 27, 2025, at Princeton High School, we believe that every student has the potential to excel in mathematics and science, and our program provides the platform to discover, nurture, and celebrate that potential.

Competition Overview

PSE is a single-day individual mathematics competition and STEM workshop session designed specifically for elementary and middle school students (grades 3-8). Our competition challenges students with engaging mathematical problems while creating an environment where participants can test their skills and connect with peers who share their passion for both mathematics and STEM.

What Makes PSE Unique

  • Individual Focus: Every student competes individually, showcasing their personal mathematical abilities
  • Inclusive Environment: Students of all skill levels are welcome, with problems designed for various experience levels
  • Real-World Applications: Our problems connect mathematical concepts to practical, real-world scenarios
  • Academic Excellence: Experience a high-quality mathematical competition in an inspiring academic setting

Competition Structure

Our mathematics competition consists of individual rounds carefully designed to challenge students while ensuring fair participation across all skill levels.

Individual Rounds

  • Sprint Round: 30 quick-fire problems testing fundamental math skills and computational speed
  • Target Round: 8 challenging problems requiring deeper mathematical reasoning and problem-solving strategies

Grade Divisions

  • Elementary Division: Grades 3-5
  • Middle School Division: Grades 6-8
