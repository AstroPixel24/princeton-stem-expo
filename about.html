About PSE

Discover our mission, vision, and commitment to STEM education excellence

Saturday, September 27, 2025
Princeton High School

Our Mission

The Princeton STEM Expo (PSE) is launching as an inaugural event dedicated to inspiring and empowering the next generation of STEM leaders through engaging mathematics competitions, innovative workshops, and meaningful connections with industry professionals. We believe that every student has the potential to excel in STEM fields, and our event provides the platform to discover, nurture, and celebrate that potential.

Event Overview

PSE is launching as an inaugural, full-day STEM-focused event taking place on Saturday, September 27, 2025, at Princeton High School. Designed specifically for elementary and middle school students, our comprehensive program combines competitive mathematics with hands-on learning experiences, creating an environment where students can challenge themselves academically while building lasting connections with peers who share their passion for STEM.

What Makes PSE Unique

  • Comprehensive STEM Focus: While mathematics competitions are at our core, we integrate science, technology, and engineering elements throughout the event
  • Inclusive Environment: Students of all skill levels are welcome, with competitions and workshops designed for various experience levels
  • Real-World Applications: Our activities connect academic concepts to practical, real-world problem-solving
  • Professional Networking: Students interact with STEM professionals, researchers, and industry leaders
  • University Setting: Experience college-level academics in the prestigious Princeton University environment

Competition Structure

Our mathematics competitions are carefully designed to challenge students while ensuring fair and engaging participation for all skill levels.

Individual Rounds

  • Sprint Round: 30 quick-fire problems testing fundamental math skills and speed
  • Target Round: 8 challenging problems requiring deeper mathematical reasoning
  • Team Round: Collaborative problem-solving with teams of 4 students
  • Countdown Round: Head-to-head competition for top performers

Grade Divisions

  • Elementary School Division: Grades 3-5
  • Middle School Division: Grades 6-8
  • Team Competitions: Mixed-grade teams allowed

Educational Impact

PSE goes beyond competition to create lasting educational value for all participants:

  • Skill Development: Enhance problem-solving, critical thinking, and analytical skills
  • Confidence Building: Gain confidence in mathematical abilities through supportive competition
  • Peer Learning: Learn from and collaborate with like-minded students
  • Career Inspiration: Discover STEM career paths through speaker presentations and workshops
  • Academic Motivation: Develop enthusiasm for continued STEM learning

Innovation & Technology

We embrace cutting-edge technology and innovative teaching methods to enhance the learning experience:

  • Digital Platforms: Modern competition software and real-time scoring systems
  • Interactive Workshops: Hands-on activities using latest STEM tools and technologies
  • Virtual Reality Experiences: Immersive STEM demonstrations and simulations
  • AI and Machine Learning: Workshops on emerging technologies shaping the future
  • Sustainable Practices: Environmentally conscious event management and STEM applications

Inclusivity & Accessibility

PSE is committed to creating an inclusive environment where all students can participate and thrive:

  • Financial Accessibility: Scholarships and reduced fees available for students with financial need
  • Accommodations: Support for students with disabilities or special learning needs
  • Diverse Perspectives: Speakers and workshop leaders from varied backgrounds and experiences
  • Gender Equity: Special initiatives to encourage participation from underrepresented groups
  • Multilingual Support: Resources available for non-native English speakers

Event History

Since its inception, PSE has grown from a small local competition to a premier regional STEM event:

  • 2020: First annual PSE with 150 participants from 20 schools
  • 2021: Expanded to include virtual participation options during global challenges
  • 2022: Introduced workshop series and increased to 300 participants
  • 2023: Added keynote speaker program and reached 450 participants
  • 2024: Record-breaking year with 500+ participants from 50+ schools
  • 2025: Expanding further with new competition categories and enhanced STEM focus

Awards & Recognition

We celebrate achievement and recognize excellence across multiple categories:

Individual Awards

  • Top 10 finishers in each division receive medals and certificates
  • Outstanding Problem Solver Award for creative solutions
  • Most Improved Participant recognition
  • Leadership in STEM Award for peer mentorship

Team Awards

  • Winning teams receive trophies and team certificates
  • Best Collaboration Award for exceptional teamwork
  • Innovation Challenge winners receive special recognition

School Recognition

  • Top performing schools receive institutional awards
  • Outstanding Participation awards for schools with high engagement
  • STEM Excellence recognition for comprehensive programs

Future Vision

Looking ahead, PSE continues to evolve and expand our impact on STEM education:

  • National Expansion: Plans to expand beyond the regional level to serve students nationwide
  • Year-Round Programming: Development of ongoing workshops and mentorship programs
  • Industry Partnerships: Deeper collaboration with STEM companies and research institutions
  • Teacher Development: Professional development opportunities for STEM educators
  • Alumni Network: Building connections between past participants and current students
