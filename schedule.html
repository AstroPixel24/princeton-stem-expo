Competition Details

Mathematics Competition Structure

  • Individual Test: 60 minutes, designed for grade-appropriate challenge
  • Format: Multiple choice and short answer problems
  • Topics: Algebra, geometry, number theory, and problem-solving

Grade Divisions

  • Elementary Division: Grades 3-5
  • Middle School Division: Grades 6-8

Special Features

  • Po-Shen Loh Presentations: Renowned mathematician and educator
  • Interactive Workshops: Hands-on STEM learning experiences
  • Awards Recognition: Top performers in each division

Scoring & Awards

  • Top 10 finishers in each division receive medals
  • Workshop participation certificates
  • Special recognition for outstanding achievement

Important Information

What to Bring

  • Photo ID or school identification
  • Calculator (scientific calculator recommended, graphing calculators allowed)
  • Pencils and erasers
  • Comfortable clothing for the competition day
  • Water bottle (water stations available)

What We Provide

  • All competition materials and answer sheets
  • Pens and pencils if needed

Health & Safety

  • Emergency contact information required during registration
  • We will attempt to accommodate any food allergies and dietary restrictions
  • Parent/guardian contact information required for all participants
